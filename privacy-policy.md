# Privacy Policy for MaciDoc – PDF Scanner

**Effective date:** July 2026

MaciDoc – PDF Scanner ("the App") is developed and published by **NexForge Labs**.

## Information Collection

MaciDoc is designed with privacy in mind.

The App does **not** collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information.

The App does not require user accounts or registration.

## Documents and Photos

All scanned documents, images, and generated PDF files remain on your device.

The App does not upload your documents to any server or cloud service.

## Permissions

The App requests only the permissions necessary for its functionality.

### Camera

Used to capture document images.

### Storage

Used to save PDF documents to your device and allow you to open or share them.

## Sharing

When you choose to share a document, the selected PDF is shared only with the application or service you explicitly choose using Android's sharing menu.

The App does not transmit files automatically.

## Third-Party Services

MaciDoc does not use analytics, advertising, tracking, or third-party data collection services.

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be published on this page with an updated effective date.
